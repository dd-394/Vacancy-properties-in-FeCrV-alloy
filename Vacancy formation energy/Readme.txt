	The directory "Vacancy formation energy" contains the raw data of model with a vacancy. The raw data includes structural information, energetical information, force information written in OUTCAR format. The OUTCAR-rlx indicates the relaxation from vacancy model creation without realxation to full relaxation with cut-off energy equaling 350 eV. For each relaxation calculation, the input structure is provided in the POSCAR-rlx file, and the fully relaxed structure is output in the CONTCAR file. The structure orignated from relaxation with cut-off energy equaling 350 eV was furtherly used to be calculated. The Self-consistent calculation with fixing structure obtained from  relaxation was performed. And the results were integrated into OUTCAR-rlx-scf. The CONTCAR file from the relaxation step is actually identical to the POSCAR file used as input for the self-consistent calculation, as the structure remains fixed during the SCF step. Although structural information can also be extracted from the OUTCAR format files, I have chosen to parse the POSCAR/CONTCAR format in my Python script for structural information extraction due to its simplicity and direct representation of the atomic coordinates.  Noticeably, the force information from OUTCAR-rlx-scf indicates that the structure relaxed with cut off energy 350 eV is also in full relaxation with cut off energy 400 eV. Herein, the vacancy formation energy is calculated based on the energetical information of OUTCAR-rlx-scf, while the relaxation process is analysed based on the structural information and force information of OUTCAR-rlx.


Note: Structural information referes to the atomic positions of vacancy model without any relaxation and the atomic position in full relaxation. Force information refers to the force vectors on atom of vacancy model without any relaxation.
